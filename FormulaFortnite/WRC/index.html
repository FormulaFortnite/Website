What is the WRC?

The World Rally Championship (WRC) is the most extreme and unpredictable form of racing within Formula Fortnite.

Combining speed, precision, and control on some of the toughest terrains, the WRC brings drivers into a dynamic rally-style competition, where every second counts.

With unique challenges, no two races are ever the same. From muddy tracks to snow-covered roads, drivers must adapt, push boundaries, and showcase true skill to conquer the course.

Whether you're here to spectate or compete, the WRC guarantees intense action and breathtaking moments every round.
