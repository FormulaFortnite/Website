What is the WRC


The World Rally Championship (WRC) is a thrilling racing series within Formula Fortnite, where drivers tackle time-based rally stages across challenging and dynamic terrains. From tight turns to unpredictable weather, this format tests every aspect of a racer's skill.

The WRC series focuses on speed, consistency, and adaptability as drivers compete to set the best stage times. Unlike standard races, WRC is all about beating the clock, not direct opponents.
