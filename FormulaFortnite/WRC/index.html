What is the WRC?

The World Rally Championship (WRC) is the most extreme and unpredictable form of racing within Formula Fortnite.

Combining speed, precision, and control on some of the toughest terrains, the WRC brings drivers into a dynamic rally-style competition, where every second counts. The races take place on various surfaces including gravel, tarmac, and snow, each offering its own set of challenges.

Rallying is a test of both endurance and mental fortitude, as drivers race against the clock rather than each other. Every race is a unique experience where the weather and road conditions can drastically change the outcome.

Whether you are a seasoned motorsport fan or a newcomer to the world of rallying, the WRC offers exciting, heart-pounding action, unpredictable moments, and fierce rivalries. The combination of these factors makes the WRC one of the most exciting motorsport competitions in the world.

Drivers from all over the world come together to showcase their skills in the ultimate test of both man and machine. The WRC season is a thrilling spectacle that keeps fans on the edge of their seats from start to finish.
