What is Formula Fortnite?


Welcome to Formula Fortnite, the most adrenaline-fueled racing series in the world of Fortnite! Prepare to experience the thrills of high-octane racing, head-to-head competition, and heart-pounding challenges. Test your driving skills and battle for the title of champion in a one-of-a-kind experience. Are you ready to tear up the track? Join us and prove you're the best!

How is Formula Fortnite structured?


Formula Fortnite is organized into three categories: FF1, FF2, and FF3. FF1 is the main series, where the strongest drivers race and the overall champion is decided. Why should I join Formula Fortnite?


Joining Formula Fortnite means entering a unique championship, where every driver has the opportunity to get involved, regardless of their experience level. Our league is inclusive, welcoming both beginners and experts. We offer a stimulating and competitive, but also fun, environment, with the possibility of growing and improving season after season. Furthermore, Formula Fortnite offers customized graphics that make each race even more exciting and engaging. Each event is designed to give participants a unique visual experience, which makes the championship even more special. Participating means being part of a dynamic community, where the passion for cars and Fortnite meets in an atmosphere of healthy competition. If you are looking for a place to challenge yourself, have fun and grow, Formula Fortnite is the right place for you.
